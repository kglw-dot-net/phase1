Live In Sydney ’21 (2021)

Recorded live at The Enmore Theatre, Cadigal country (Sydney), Australia, April 22nd 2021 and released May 29, 2021, this release was also made available on Youtube by the band with concert footage. The performance occurred shortly after the release of L.W. and has an entirely microtonal set-list comprised with the other two volumes of that series and some rarities from Sketches Of Brunswick East.
