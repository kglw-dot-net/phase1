KGLW.net

Willoughby’s Beach (2011)

The precursor to debut album 12 Bar Bruise, this EP is an even rougher blend of Surf-Punk with little of the psychedelic elements KGATLW would become known for. Named after a beach local to some of the group members, it shows the roots of the group’s formation as an underground party band.

What to listen to next:

  • If you want to continue with the evolution of the band
  • If you want to hear more rough early material
