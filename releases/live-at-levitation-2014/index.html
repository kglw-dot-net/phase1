Live At Levitation ’14 (2021)

Recorded live at Carson Creek Ranch, Austin, Texas, USA, May 2nd 2014 and released December 10, 2021. This was the first full live show that KGATLW played in the United States, at the psychedelic rock festival Levitation.

Promoting I’m In Your Mind Fuzz at the time, the set list consists mostly of that album, including the rare b-side Wholly Ghost (this is the only digital publication of that song), and concluding with Head On – Pill (Float Along — Fill Your Lungs).
