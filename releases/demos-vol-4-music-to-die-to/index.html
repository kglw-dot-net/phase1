Demos Vol. 4 – Music To Die To (2022)

A compilation of Demos, released alongside Demos Vol. 3 – Music To Eat Pond Scum To. Contains early versions of published songs and unnamed/abandoned concepts recorded between 2011-2021. This volume contains early demos of songs from: Flying Microtonal Banana, Murder of the Universe, Fishing For Fishies, Omnium Gatherum, and Nonagon Infinity.
