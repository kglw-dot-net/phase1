Demos Vol. 3 – Music To Eat Pond Scum To

Release Date: 15 July 2022

A compilation of Demos, released alongside Demos Vol. 4 – Music To Die To. Contains early versions of published songs and unnamed/abandoned concepts recorded between 2011-2021. This volume contains early demos of songs from: Butterfly 3000, Gumboot Soup, Infest the Rats Nest, K.G., and Omnium Gatherum and Willoughby’s Beach.
