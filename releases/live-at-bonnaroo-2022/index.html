Live At Bonnaroo ’22 (2022)

Recorded live at Bonnaroo, Great Stage Park, Manchester, TN, USA, 17th June 2022, and released August 2022. This set contains staples from Nonagon Infinity, Infest The Rats Nest and I’m In Your Mind Fuzz, mixed with choices from the newly released Omnium Gatherum (including rare live vocals from Cook for his track, The Garden Goblin). The bootlegger package includes the professionally shot concert footage from the festival stream.
