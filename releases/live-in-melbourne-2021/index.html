Live In Melbourne ’21 (2021)

Recorded live at Summer Sound Festival, Sidney Myer Music Bowl, Naarm (Melbourne), Australia, February 26, 2021 and released March 19th 2021, this release was also made available on Youtube by the band with concert footage. Opening with a rare solo drumming track titled ‘Fury’, the performance occurred between the releases of companion albums K.G. and L.W. with both heavily featured. Several live staples from Flying Microtonal Banana are also included to round out this entirely microtonal set-list.
