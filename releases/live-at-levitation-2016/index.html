Live At Levitation ’16 (2021)

Recorded live at Carson Creek Ranch, Austin, Texas, USA, May 1st 2016 and released December 10, 2021, this is the return of KGATLW to psychedelic rock festival Levitation. They were building up to the release of Nonagon Infinity at the time, playing songs from that album mixed with its predecessor I’m In Your Mind Fuzz, featuring live staple The River (Quarters!) and concluding with a rare version of Cut Throat Boogie (12 Bar Bruise)
