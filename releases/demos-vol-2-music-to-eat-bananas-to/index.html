Demos Vol. 2 – Music To Eat Bananas To (2020)

A compilation of Demos, released alongside Vol. 1 – Music To Kill Bad People To. Contains early versions of published songs and unnamed/abandoned concepts recorded between 2011-2020. This volume contains early demos of songs from: Sketches of Brunswick East, Paper Mâché Dream Balloon, Willoughby’s Beach, Polygondwanaland, K.G., and Oddments.
